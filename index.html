About us

CWW Tech Africa is empowering young people with digital and tech skills. CWW Tech Africa was created to empower African youths to be employable and enterpreneurial. Our graduates become mentors, contribute to the CWW Tech Africa community, the tech ecosystem and the world.

Frequently Asked Questions

How do i join CWW Tech Africa program

To join the program, click on "Apply as a trainee" on the homepage at www.cwwtechafrica.com

Is the certificate free?

No, certificate fee is #2500 0r 6$ upon completion of the program.

Will there be internship placement after the program?

Intrenships will be awarded on basis of performance and commitment to the program.

Is the CTA training program free?

The CTA training program is absolutely free for all African youths residing in Africa. However, you are required to pay a non-refundable commitment fee of #2000 or 5$.

Non-Africans interested in the progrsm will pay a commitment fee of 10$.

We request for commitment fee to ensure that you complete the program.

How long is the CWW Tech Africa program?

Duration of program is 6 weeks.
